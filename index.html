About Me

And some of my practices

I am an Oklahoma native and a former D1 athlete. I am passionate about healthy living and dedicated to helping others achieve their fitness goals. My specialties include personal training, yoga, and nutritional guidance.

My holistic approach to wellness combines traditional and alternative practices to help clients reach their full potential. With my fitness knowledge, endurance, and energy, I strive to motivate and inspire others to make positive and lasting changes in their lives.

Benefits of choosing Fit One

Fit One offers the best of both worlds: personalized attention and expertise from a certified trainer, combined with an inviting and supportive atmosphere.

I believe in creating an individualized experience for each of my clients, with tailored training programs and a focus on accountability and progress. With my state-of-the-art equipment and supportive atmosphere, I make sure that every visit to my studio is a positive one. So if you're looking for a personalized exercise experience, come to Fit One and see why it's the best choice for your fitness needs.
